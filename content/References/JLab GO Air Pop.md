---
title: JLab GO Air Pop
---
## Left

- Vol Down: `Single Tap`
- Assistant: `Double Tap`
- Track Back: `Press and Hold 1+ sec`

## Right

- Vol Up: `Single Tap`
- Play / Pause: `Double Tap`
- Track Fwd: `Press and Hold 1+ sec`

## Either

- Answer Call: `Single Tap`
- Hangup: `Double Tap`
- Reject Call: `Press and Hold 1+ sec`
- EQ Change: `Triple Tap`
